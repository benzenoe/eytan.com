#### The Commoditization of Basic Services

As AI approaches “Automated AI Research” capabilities (roughly 2026–2027), many traditional service roles will be redefined or hollowed out.

* Real estate agents who simply “stick it on the MLS and wait” will be replaced.
* Salespeople using generic scripts will lose to AI systems that personalize at scale and speed.

If your value proposition can be templated and automated, it will be.

#### The Trust Paradox

As AI-generated content becomes indistinguishable from human creation, *trust* becomes both harder to earn and more valuable than ever.

Inside **REIGNation** (**[https://reignation.com](https://reignation.com)**) this is already a core topic:

* How do you prove *real* expertise when AI can simulate expert language?
* How do you demonstrate judgment, experience, and consistency AI cannot fake?

The answer: transparency, track record, and behavior over time.
AI can mimic a paragraph. It cannot mimic your lived history.

#### Economic Displacement

The progression toward potential “Superintelligence” raises real questions:

* What happens to knowledge workers as AI becomes faster, cheaper, and often better?
* What forms of work become *premium* because they remain human, rare, and embodied?

This isn’t doom—it’s restructuring. Those who adapt early win.
